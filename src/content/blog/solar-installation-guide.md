# Solar Installation Guide

Welcome to the ultimate guide on solar installation!

Solar energy is one of the most sustainable ways to power your home. In this article, we'll walk you through the key steps involved in a typical solar panel installation.

## Why Solar?
Solar power reduces your electricity bills and carbon footprint. It's a win-win for you and the planet.

## Key Steps
1. Site assessment
2. Design and planning
3. Permitting and approvals
4. Installation and testing
5. Final inspection and grid connection
